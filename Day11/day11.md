## What is an ATS Match Score?

An ATS (Applicant Tracking System) Match Score estimates how well a resume aligns with a specific job description.

The score is based on factors such as:

- Relevant technical keywords
- Skills matching
- Resume structure
- Section formatting
- Action-oriented language
- Recruiter readability

A higher ATS Match Score generally means the resume is more likely to pass the initial screening process and reach a recruiter.

---

## Why Job Description (JD) Matching is Important

Every job posting contains important keywords, required skills, and responsibilities.

By tailoring a resume to match the Job Description, candidates can:

- Improve ATS compatibility.
- Increase the chances of passing automated screening.
- Highlight the most relevant skills and projects.
- Present their experience in a way that matches employer expectations.

The goal is not to add false information but to organize and present existing experience more effectively.
