🔍 COMMON ATS PITFALLS TO AVOID

❌ DON'T use tables, text boxes, or graphics (even your SVG portfolios) → ATS can't parse visual elements
❌ DON'T use fancy fonts, unusual colors, or special characters (except hyphens, periods, colons)
❌ DON'T include header/footer images → ATS often ignores them
❌ DON'T use more than one page (unless you have 5+ years of experience) → Keep it concise
❌ DON'T repeat the same job title multiple times → Vary section headers
❌ DON'T list every tool/framework used → Focus on 8-12 most relevant per role

✅ DO keep it scannable: clear headers, bullet points, consistent formatting
✅ DO use standard fonts (Calibri, Arial, Times New Roman)
✅ DO include relevant keywords from the job description
✅ DO add metrics and quantifiable results (%, $, latency, users, accuracy, etc.)
✅ DO use action verbs at the start of bullets (Built, Engineered, Developed, Shipped, Deployed)
