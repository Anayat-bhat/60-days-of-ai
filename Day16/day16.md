# Day 16 – Custom Skills: Stock Fundamental Research

## Objective

The objective of today's task was to learn how to create a reusable **Custom Skill** in Claude that performs structured stock fundamental analysis. Instead of entering the same detailed prompt every time, I created a permanent workflow that can generate comprehensive research reports for different companies with a single request.

---

# What are Custom Skills?

Custom Skills are reusable AI workflows that allow Claude to remember a specific set of instructions. Once a skill is created, it can be used across multiple conversations without rewriting the prompt.

They help automate repetitive tasks, improve consistency, and make AI much more efficient for frequently used workflows.

---

# What is the Stock Fundamental Research Skill?

The **Stock Fundamental Research** skill is designed to analyze Indian and global listed companies using their financial fundamentals rather than short-term price movements.

The skill evaluates a company's financial performance, profitability, valuation, balance sheet strength, ownership structure, business quality, competitive advantages, risks, and future growth prospects. It produces structured, evidence-based reports while avoiding investment advice or buy/sell recommendations.

---

# Why are Custom Skills Useful?

Creating Custom Skills offers several advantages:

- Eliminates the need to repeatedly paste long prompts.
- Produces consistent and well-structured outputs.
- Saves time when performing similar tasks.
- Improves productivity by automating common workflows.
- Makes AI more reliable for research and professional work.
- Allows the same workflow to be reused indefinitely.
