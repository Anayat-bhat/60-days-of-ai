📋 Quarterly Growth Strategy Meeting

Transcript Mode — structured breakdown of decisions, action items, risks, and open questions

🧾 Summary

The leadership team reviewed Q2 performance: revenue grew 12%, below the 18% target, mainly due to 5 enterprise deals slipping (procurement delays, added security reviews). Website traffic rose 34% but conversion remains weak, prompting a planned redesign — though this competes with engineering's August analytics dashboard deadline. Customer retention is strong (94%) but support tickets are rising due to reporting performance and dashboard loading issues, which the CEO flagged as a priority. Hiring plans and the customer conference budget were both postponed pending further inputs. No public commitment will be made on the August dashboard release date until engineering completes its milestone review.
