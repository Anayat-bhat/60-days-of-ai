# What is CSV Data Analysis?

CSV (Comma-Separated Values) is one of the most commonly used formats for storing structured data. It is widely used in business analytics, finance, operations, and data science because it is simple to read and process.

Claude can analyze CSV files, calculate statistics, identify trends, compare categories, and generate reports without requiring any programming.

---

# Why Data Analysis is Important

Data analysis helps convert raw information into meaningful insights that support better decision-making.

Instead of looking at hundreds of rows of data manually, AI can quickly identify:

- Cost patterns
- Performance trends
- Business metrics
- Environmental impact
- Financial comparisons
- Areas for improvement

This makes data easier to understand and communicate.

# Key Insights from the Analysis

The analysis highlighted several useful insights:

- Different fuel types have significantly different running costs.
- Maintenance expenses increase as vehicle age increases.
- Environmental impact varies depending on the fuel used.
- Monthly operating cost depends on both fuel price and driving distance.
- Break-even calculations provide a better understanding of long-term fuel economics rather than focusing only on fuel price.

These insights demonstrate how data can support more informed decisions.
