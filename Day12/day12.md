## What is Personal Branding?

Personal branding is the process of presenting yourself professionally so that recruiters, employers, and other professionals understand who you are, what you can do, and what makes you different.

It is more than just having a resume. It includes your LinkedIn profile, portfolio website, GitHub projects, communication style, and the way you present your skills and achievements.

A strong personal brand helps create trust and leaves a positive impression on recruiters.

---

## Why Recruiter Outreach is Important

Many opportunities come through networking rather than online applications alone.

Sending a professional message to recruiters or hiring managers helps introduce yourself, express your interest in a role, and increase your chances of getting noticed.

A well-written outreach message demonstrates professionalism and can open doors to interviews, referrals, and networking opportunities.

---

## What is a Cover Letter?

A cover letter is a professional document that accompanies a resume when applying for a job.

It explains why you are interested in the position, highlights your most relevant skills and projects, and shows how your experience matches the job requirements.

Unlike a resume, which lists qualifications, a cover letter tells your professional story and explains why you are a suitable candidate.

---

## Why LinkedIn Networking Matters

LinkedIn is one of the most important platforms for students and professionals.

Networking on LinkedIn allows you to:

- Connect with recruiters and industry professionals.
- Discover internship and job opportunities.
- Share projects and technical achievements.
- Build a professional online presence.
- Learn from experienced engineers and AI professionals.

Maintaining an active LinkedIn profile helps improve visibility and credibility.
