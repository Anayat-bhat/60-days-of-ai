# Day 16 – Custom Skills: Stock Fundamental Research

## Objective

Learn how to create and use a Custom Skill in Claude to perform structured stock fundamental analysis without repeatedly entering the same long prompt.

---

## What are Custom Skills?

Custom Skills are reusable workflows in Claude that allow users to save detailed prompts as permanent capabilities. Instead of copying and pasting the same instructions every time, the skill can be selected and used in any new conversation. This saves time, improves consistency, and makes repetitive tasks much more efficient.

---

## What is the Stock Fundamental Research Skill?

The Stock Fundamental Research Skill is a reusable workflow that analyzes listed companies using their financial fundamentals. It generates an organized report covering company performance, financial health, valuation, ownership, growth, strengths, risks, and other important business metrics.

The skill focuses only on factual financial information and does not provide buy, sell, or hold recommendations.

---

## Why Custom Skills are Useful

- Save time by avoiding repeated prompts.
- Maintain consistent report quality.
- Standardize the analysis process.
- Improve productivity for frequently performed tasks.
- Easily reuse the same workflow across multiple conversations.
